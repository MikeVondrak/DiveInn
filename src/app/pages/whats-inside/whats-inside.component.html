INSIDE THE DIVE INN

Dive Bar • Sports Bar • Games Bar

Dive Inn is one of Southeast Denver's most popular venues, with a cozy dive bar, a big-screen sports bar, and a lively games bar, all in the same great space!

With awesome drink specials every day and a huge variety of crazy events throughout the year, there's always a reason to visit the Dive Inn.

Open 365 days a year, we're ready whenever you are!

HUGE SPACE

6300 square feet of adult playground means there's always plenty of space for you and all your friends.

THREE ROOMS

The large front room houses the main bar and a seating area with a wall of TVs, not to mention a 18' speedboat you can Wii fish from, and two different game rooms in back offer pool, foosball, and FREE cornhole and table tennis.

THREE PATIOS

A giant interior patio space hosts table tennis and cornhole games, while two satellite patios provide plenty of seating to enjoy the great Colorado weather.

WIDE OPEN

How best to compliment a giant bar with 3 rooms and 3 patios? How about 5 garage doors, one opening to each patio, making Dive Inn the perfect summer spot.

13 WIDESCREENS

Whatever sporting event you want to catch you can be sure it will be playing on one of the Dive Inn's 13 high definition flatscreens, with MLB, NHL, and NFL DIRECTV sports packages showing games from across the country.

THREE BARS

One main bar keeps the crowd properly lubricated, and two additional bars can open up for when things get truely crazy.

PRIVATE PARTIES

Charter your own 18' party boat for a group of up to 13, or reserve a whole room, patio, or bar and even your own private tap!

DIVE INN GALLEY

Serving up authentic Mexican favorites from our own in-house taqueria, the Dive Inn Galley has something for every appetite.

HABITUAL RELAPSE

Occurring weekly and seasonally, these special events offer a great variety of ways to enjoy a different side of Dive Inn...

Detox to Retox

Come practice your Downward Dog and enjoy a bit of the hair of it, too. Yoga class and bottomless mimosas on Saturday mornings.

Itsy Bitsy Teeny Weeny All You Can Drink...

Ladies, don you tanning attire and enjoy free drinks on the patio sun lounge, Saturdays during summer.

Sunday Funday

Donate to a great cause and get a free t-shirt and drink specials, every Sunday.

Crawfish Boil

Get your fill of real Cajun cuisine, held throughout the summer.
